Questions

Everything you'd ask before trusting it with your health.

Is Aévrion a replacement for a doctor?

No. Aévrion triages, explains, and routes — every meaningful decision still goes to a licensed specialist. Think of it as the fastest, most thorough intake nurse you've ever had, available at 2 a.m.

Who can see my health records?

Only you, and any doctor you explicitly share a record with for a specific consultation. Records are encrypted at rest and in transit, and access is logged.

What happens if the AI gets a triage wrong?

Every AI suggestion is a starting point for a human clinician, not a final diagnosis, and severity estimates always err toward caution. If something looks urgent, we tell you to seek care immediately rather than wait for confirmation.

Can hospitals integrate their own systems?

Yes — the Hospital plan connects to most major EHR systems and lets your own clinicians review and override any AI-generated recommendation.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, from your account settings, with no retention calls or hidden steps. Your records remain exportable for 12 months after cancellation.
